Elotes

Description

This will be the most flavorful corn you've ever tasted!

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 1/4 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup of Cotija cheese, crumbled
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed or finely minced
  • 1/4tsp fine sea salt
  • Juice and zest of 1 lime
  • 1/4 of cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1/2tsp chipotle chili powder

Steps

  1. Heat grill to 400F
  2. Place corn on grill for roughly 12 minutes. 1/4 turn every 3 minutes
  3. Mix topping sauce by stirring together sour cream, mayonnaise, cotija cheese, garlic, lime zest and juice, and cilantro.
  4. Generously top each corn cob with cheese mixture, sprinkle with a bit of chipotle chili powder, and top with extra cotija and cilantro.
