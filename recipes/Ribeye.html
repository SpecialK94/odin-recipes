Grandpa's Ribeye

Description

Visiting my grandparents for dinner has always been one of my favorite ways to spend an evening.

This experience is made even more special when I get to eat one of his perfectly cooked Ribeye. Below is how it's done.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crust outside of steak with salt, pepper, and garlic salt
  2. Sprinkle additional seasoning on top and bottom of steak
  3. Grill at 500F for three minutes on each side
  4. Use flames from the steak drippings to sear for 30 seconds on each side
  5. Rest wrapped in foil for five minutes prior to serving
